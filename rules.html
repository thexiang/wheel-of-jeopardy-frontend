Introduction

Wheel of Jeopardy is a game combining various elements of well know T.V. game shows Jeopardy and Wheel of Fortune. Player’s will answer questions to score points, the questions to be answered are determined by the spin of a wheel containing sections corresponding to question categories.

Gameplay

The game begins with Player 1 spining the wheel by pressing the spin button. The result of the spin will then detrimine the players next step.
The possible spin results are as follows
1. A category, The player will be prompted to select and answer one of the available questions on the question board. They will then answer the question aloud to there opponent. The opponent will then select if the player was correct or not. If the player is correct, they are awarded the questions point value. If the player is incorrect the questions point value is subtracted from the players score.
2. Double Score, The players score is doubled even if negative
3. Free Turn, The player is awarded a free turn token that can be used to void a Lose a turn spin or spin again after answering a question incorrectly.
4. Lose Turn, Player Loses a turn
5. Bankruptcy, Players score is set to 0
6. Player Choice, The player can select any available question category to answer from.
7. Opponents Choice, The player’s opponent can select any available question category for the player to answer a question from.
After the player's spin is complete and the action associated with the spin has been completed the players turn is over and it is the next players turn to spin. This is repeated until all of the questions have been answered.
The winner of the game is the player with the most points after all of the questions have been answered.
